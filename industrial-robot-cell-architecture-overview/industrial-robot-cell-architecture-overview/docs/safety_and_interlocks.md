# Safety and Interlocks

## Main idea

Safety is not just an add-on. It defines whether the cell is allowed to operate at all.

## Typical safety elements

- emergency stops
- door / gate switches
- enabling devices
- safety relays or safety PLC logic
- robot safety status
- reset requirements

## Interlocks vs safety

### Safety
Determines whether motion is permitted at a fundamental level.

### Interlocks
Determine whether a particular machine action is logically allowed.

Both matter, but they are not identical.

## Why this matters

A cell should clearly define:
- what blocks motion
- what blocks auto cycle
- what requires reset
- what faults require operator intervention
